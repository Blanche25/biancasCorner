ALAS! AN ORIGINAL PIECE I AM HAPPY WITH!
this one took a bit longer than i anticipated, i started writing it in september and only now got around to finishing it. i got inspired to make this after listening to Hirokazu Sato's Waltz of North Wind (if you've never heard of Hirokazu Sato, do check out his compositions, they're some of my favorites to play), if you listen to it you might even recognize the main two chords i borrowed.

all in all, flows better than i thought, and it's pretty fun to play.
